Growing Up; Apart From Your Resemblance.

Gospel of Matthew

Genealogy

My paternal grandfather is Ebenezer Omotosho Adeniji; and my biological father is Jimmy Adeniji; but I grew up with my paternal uncle, Jim Adeniji and his wife, my aunt-in-law, Ayodele Adeniji, nee Falua; who was born on 1945-12-01, and passed away on 1988-04-01. There are thirty years between 1988-04-01 and 2018-04-01.

Date and Place of Birth

My twin sibling and I, were born on 1967-10-15, at Lagos Island Maternity Hospital. 10 Campbell Street, Lagos Island, Lagos State, Nigeria.

Biological Parents Residence

My biological parents resided at 2 Shipeolu Street. Palm Groove, Lagos State, Nigeria. A place they rented from the Oyeniya family.

Nativity

I am a native of Ile-Ife, the Yoruba cradle; and I am a member of the Ilare royal family.

Primary School

I attended Staff School, University of Ile-Ife. My three classmates that I recall are Wumi, a male friend, who also lived in my neighborhood, and we later travelled to school with; Bimbo, a female, who I once fought with; and Stephen, a foreigner.
