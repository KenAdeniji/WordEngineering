Abstract

This doctorate research project, is an attempt to find the man, we are meant to be. The Bible, specifically King James Version (KJV), will be studied, to broaden, our scope of knowledge.
The problems we will address, include, the rules, changing man.
The method of solving these problems are scripture reading, modelling, object oriented programming, relational database, and artificial intelligence. The conclusion reached, is that man is made, in the image of God, and he will succumb to Him. Man at a compassionate end, in composing his future. The design effort, is to appreciate. Half of you, I made up. Half for Me (Genesis 2:7, Genesis 2:22-23).

The author believes, that by following the orders, lived in the Bible, he can gain an understanding, on how-to live life, according to God's commandments. Man has a life time, to abide by God's will. Theory

To start off, the author assume, that he will have to read the entire Bible, and find cases, where God has led man. However, this will be laborious, and will cover a lot of information overload, that may be unnecessary. Therefore, a computer program, earlier written by the author that will list the first, last, and number of occurrences of the term, lead, was consulted. A table, was created in a relational database to store the initial entries.

At this stage of the game, it is unknown, how valuable this will be, reading data in a document is easier than getting information off a database. We will show the table schema, data definition language, and meta data (data about data). This may be useful for the people, that are database literate, and will like an expedition in this matter. Programmers may also like to read information from the database, in their constructs, and we will make room for this. For the non-too computer literate, we will break information down into easy to read, English grammar.
