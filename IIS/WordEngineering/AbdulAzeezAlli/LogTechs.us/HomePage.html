Overview

LogTechs provides HIT and HIM solutions. We provide the complete lifecycle of Eletronic Medical Record (EMR) systems. LogTechs specializes in application integration and data, and infrastructure throughout IT lifecycle. Our services include staffing for command centers, help desks for first-point of resolution; in-class and one-on-one training for physicians and nurses, go-lives to optimization.
