The ng-init directive attribute, sets default values, for example, ng-init="qty=1;cost=2", initializes the qty and cost defined in this scope as input fields.
Expressions and filters are enclosed between two braces, on both sides. Whenever one of the expression dependencies changes, the expression is re-evaluated. The filter may be the currency, which will display the amount with a currency prefix. For example, {{qty * cost | currency}}
